Flash Chat

Chatting on the go

A private group chatting application.

Simple, Reliable Messaging

Message your friends and family for free*. Flash chat uses your phone's Internet connection to send messages so you can avoid SMS fees.

Groups to keep in touch

Keep in touch with the groups of people that matter the most, like your family or coworkers. With group chats, you can share messages, photos, and videos with up to 256 people at once. You can also name your group, mute or customize notifications, and more

Share Moments that Matter

Send photos and videos on Flash chat instantly. You can even capture the moments that matter to you most with a built-in camera. With Flash chat, photos and videos send quickly even if you're on a slow connection.
